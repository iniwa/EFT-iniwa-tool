## 1. Tour

**Wiki**: https://escapefromtarkov.fandom.com/wiki/Tour

**Description (EN)**:
During the assault on the TerraGroup office, a massive explosion erupted on top of one of the nearby high-rises. I got left on my own, with no contact with command. Looks like I'll have to handle the evacuation myself. But first things first, I have to get out of this Ground Zero.

**Requirements**: ゲーム開始時に自動追加

### Objectives

#### Phase 1: Ground Zero脱出
- Escape Ground Zero

#### Phase 2: Therapist — Streets of Tarkov
- Talk to Therapist
- Hand over 250,000 Roubles to Therapist (Streets of Tarkovへのアクセス確保)
- _(Optional)_ Collect the required 250,000 Roubles

#### Phase 3: Ragman — Interchange
- Talk to Ragman
- Survive and extract from Interchange **or visit Interchange 3 times**
- Tell Ragman what you found during the recon

#### Phase 4: Skier — Customs
- Talk to Skier
- Survive and extract from Customs
- Hand over 5 "Building materials" category items (FIR) to Skier

#### Phase 5: Mechanic — Factory
- Talk to Mechanic
- Survive and extract from Factory
- Hand over 2 weapons (FIR) to Mechanic

#### Phase 6: Skier — Woods
- Talk to Skier
- Eliminate 3 targets on Woods
- Survive and extract from Woods

#### Phase 7: Terminal (Shoreline)
- Locate the Terminal entrance (Shoreline southeast)
- Find a way to contact the Terminal soldiers
- Use the intercom to contact the port garrison
- Learn about escape routes from Tarkov

#### Phase 8: マップアクセス確保
- Hand over 20,000 USD to Mechanic (Lighthouseアクセス確保)
- Hand over 5 PMC dogtags (any level) to Prapor (Reserveアクセス確保)
- Survive and extract from Shoreline

#### Phase 9: The Lab
- Enter the secret TerraGroup facility (via Transit from Streets or Factory)
- Search the top management offices
- Search the server room
- Locate the escape path through the drainage

**Rewards**: 実績「Pathfinder」解除。後続チャプターへの分岐発生。

**Description (EN)**:
Mechanic and I got to talking about that plane that went down somewhere in the nature reserve. Nobody really knows what happened to it. I should track it down and take a look — maybe I'll find something interesting.

**Requirements**: Tour進行中にMechanicと墜落機について話す

### Objectives

#### Phase 1: 墜落機の発見
- Locate the fallen plane (Woods)
- Reach Loyalty Level 2 with Prapor

#### Phase 2: トレーダー聞き込み
- Ask the traders about the fallen plane (Prapor, Therapist, Skier, Mechanic, Jaeger)
- _(Optional)_ Hand over 2,000 USD to Therapist to learn details about the SUV

#### Phase 3: G-Wagon調査
- Retrieve the flash drive from one of the G-Wagon SUVs (Shoreline)
- Hand over the flash drive to Prapor

#### Phase 4: フライトレコーダー
- Wait for information from Prapor (~1時間)
- Retrieve the plane's flight recorder (Woods)
- Leave the flight recorder in the specified spot (Shoreline, Scav Island)

#### Phase 5: 証拠品収集
- Visit Prapor and hand over required items (Toolset x2, RecBat x3, PCB x5)
- Hand over the flight crew's transcript (Chairman's House, Shoreline village)
- Hand over Elektronik's secure flash drive (同上)

#### Phase 6: 真相と選択
- Wait for information from Prapor, answer his questions (真実を伝えると報酬アップ)

  1. **Keep** the armored case for yourself → Mechanicルート (The Ticket)
  2. **Hand over** the armored case directly to Prapor → Praporルート (The Ticket)
  3. **Pretend** you didn't find it, but still hand it over to Prapor → Praporルート (The Ticket)

## 3. The Ticket

**Wiki**: https://escapefromtarkov.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ticket

**最終章。4つのエンディングに分岐する。**

### Objectives

#### Phase 1: 共通開始
- Obtain Intelligence Center level 1
- Talk to Mr. Kerman
- Wait for Mr. Kerman to reach out
- Talk to Prapor
- Talk to Mr. Kerman

#### Phase 2a: Praporルート (Falling SkiesでケースをPraporに渡した場合)
- Locate the camp of Prapor's people on Lighthouse
- Find any clues about Prapor's people's intentions
- Talk to Mr. Kerman
- Talk to Lightkeeper (gain access, stay on good terms)
- Obtain 3 TerraGroup "Blue Folders" materials
- Bring the found items to Lightkeeper
- Launch a yellow flare at ULTRA main entrance (Interchange)
- Eliminate 15 enemies on Interchange (in one raid)
- Talk to Lightkeeper and receive the Armored case

#### Phase 2b: Mechanicルート (Falling Skiesでケースを保持した場合)
- Talk to Mechanic
- Find "Experimental signal jammer" in The Lab
- Unlock the case at Workbench Lv1 to obtain the "Ticket"
- Obtain "RFID card encryption device" (Streets, Klimov 14A)
- Obtain "Master Keycard" (The Lab, Kruglov's office etc.)
- Activate RFID card at Intel Center (Craft)

#### Phase 3: Terminal入口 (共通)
- Go to Shoreline Terminal entrance and use the intercom with the card

#### Phase 4: Kermanとの選択 — エンディング分岐

**選択1: Mr. Kermanの申し出を受ける**
→ さらに分岐:

  **選択2a: TerraGroupの証拠収集に合意**
  - Agree to find dirt on TerraGroup for Mr. Kerman
  - Obtain evidence items (複数の証拠フォルダ):
    - _(Optional)_ Folder with TerraGroup evidence (Staff)
    - _(Optional)_ Folder with TerraGroup Labs evidence (Developments)
    - _(Optional)_ Folder with TerraGroup evidence (Finances)
  - 十分な証拠（3つ以上のmajor evidence）を渡す → **Savior ending**
  - 証拠が2つ以下 → **Debtor ending**

  **選択2b: 証拠収集を拒否**
  → **Survivor ending**

**選択1b: Mr. Kermanの申し出を拒否**
- Prapor tasks:
  - Eliminate any 50 targets on Streets of Tarkov
  - Eliminate 4 PMC operatives in one raid
  - Report to Prapor
  - _(失敗時)_ Hand over Secure container Kappa to Prapor
→ **Fallen ending**

#### Phase 5: Terminal脱出 (全エンディング共通)
- Pass the security check
- Locate the armory with your confiscated equipment
- Obtain the keycard to unlock the service passage
- Access the Terminal loading zone
- Locate the way into the fuel depot
- Drain the water in the pumping station
- Obtain the key to the gate blocking the road to the pier
- Locate the exit leading to the pier
- **Escape from Tarkov**

### エンディング報酬
- **Savior ending**: (Kermanに協力＋十分な証拠)
- **Debtor ending**: (Kermanに協力＋証拠不足)
- **Survivor ending**: (Kermanに協力するが証拠収集を拒否)
- **Fallen ending**: (Kermanを拒否)
